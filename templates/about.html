About the Project

Project Overview

This project focuses on the robust classification of sleep disorders using advanced Machine Learning techniques. Sleep disorders like Insomnia and Sleep Apnea affect millions globally, leading to significant health and lifestyle complications. Early, accurate diagnosis traditionally relies on expensive polysomnography tests.

AI & Machine Learning

By leveraging complex datasets consisting of physiological metrics (Heart Rate, Sleep Duration, BMI, Age, Stress Levels, etc.), our system trains powerful classification models. These AI models serve as preliminary diagnostic tools, assisting healthcare professionals in identifying key indicators of sleep abnormalities efficiently.

Goal

The ultimate goal is to provide a highly accurate, intuitive, and accessible technological interface bridging the gap between raw medical data and actionable clinical insights. This dashboard visualizes classification outcomes, model evaluation matrices, and prediction results in real-time.
